## Support policy

We provide technical support for all Arcjet users.

- **Email:** [support@arcjet.com](mailto:support@arcjet.com).
- **Discord:** [Join](https://discord.gg/TPra6jqZDC).
- **Slack:** [Email us](mailto:support@arcjet.com) and we'll spin up a private,
  shared channel.
